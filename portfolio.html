About Us

At Parker Studio, we are dedicated to the professional treatment of historic or antique objects for museums and private collections in the Delaware Valley. We specialize in furniture and musical instruments with obsolete mechanisms. We have extensive training in all facets of historic furniture construction, painting and varnishing technique, metalworking, clock making, wood and bone carving, and mechanical musical instrument building.
